About.

NLP Ventures is an investor and hands-on operational partner for portfolio companies in the Sports, Entertainment, and Technology sectors.

We serve as a founder's most trusted teammate and an investor's strongest defender. As an active board member, we provide industry expertise, strategic direction, along with access to NLP Ventures world class partners, including:

  • Investors
  • A-List Athlete and Celebrity Partners
  • Brand Builders
  • Marketing Resources
  • Media Relations
  • Sports, Entertainment & Tech Executives
  • Legal Expertise
  • M&A Opportunities

At NLP Ventures we ensure that our portfolio companies shorten their time to scale and investors maximize their investments. Led by serial entrepreneur and NLP Ventures founder Mark French, we work with CEOs and Board of Directors to innovate & solve problems at every level to help businesses grow successfully in all stages.
